About me

My first working experience was with a multinational advertising and public relations company, where I had the opportunitity to work with creative teams who introduced me to the world of Digital marketing, Graphic design, and Video production.
My goal now is to build on that experience and develop more skills through the IMD program at Algonquin College to explore new opportunities.

What I enjoyed the most about my job was meeting with my team, exploring new ideas and challenging each other to provide a better version of our work. I believe that creating an inclusive and positive work dynamics, in addition to mutual understanding is important to ensure success. Moreover, I enjoy learning new things, meeting new people and discovering new cultures.
Which is why I moved from Morocco to Canada on June 10th 2019.
